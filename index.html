What we're playing for

We compete on best-in-market technology. Premium product, sold anywhere — open to consolidating production where it makes sense. Our edge is the tech, not the local footprint.

5 Corporate Objectives

  1. Retained earnings — swing from negative back to positive.
  2. Capital structure — Equity / Liabilities ≥20/80 every quarter; target 50/50.
  3. Profitability — quarterly ROA growth ≥10%.
  4. Working capital — original target was <$500k inventory by P7; P7 close: $3.57M / 159k units (missed — new g2 stock now sits in ending inventory). Revised P8 ambition: hold flat or down while clearing g2 stock.
  5. Innovation — position in top 5% of market leaders via sustained R&D.

Operating principles

IP-led innovation
We own X1/Y1 patents. License higher grades from Clevers only when the license is cheaper than in-house R&D. Default: in-house.
Cash discipline
HQ never below $100k floor. Hunt every quarterly subsidy. Match production tightly to demand — cash > inventory.
