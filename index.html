Our binding plan, as approved 2026-05-13

We swing retained earnings from −$3.3M to +$1M+ over six quarters by running EUR as the treasury hub, defending all five plants, investing aggressively in R&D, and pricing for cash > inventory.
This is the durable corporate frame — we do not relitigate it period by period. Doc 17+ bulletins are interpreted through this plan, not against it.

5 Corporate Objectives by P10 unless noted

  1. Retained earnings — swing from −$3.3M (P4) to >+$1M by P10. ($4.3M turnaround / 6 qtrs.)
  2. Capital structure — Equity / Liabilities ≥20/80 every quarter; back to ≥50/50 by P10.
  3. Profitability — quarterly ROA growth ≥10% from P8 onward.
  4. Working capital — cut inventory from $3.3M / 232k units to <$500k by P7 (≈$2.8M cash released).
  5. Innovation — position in top 5% of market leaders via sustained R&D.

Operating principles the four rules

EUR is the treasury hub
Three operating subs (USA, EUR, JAP) + HQ Liechtenstein. EUR is the only cash-positive territory at P5 start — most upstream transfers route through EUR → HQ.
IP-led innovation
We own X1/Y1 patents. License higher grades from Clevers only when license cost < in-house R&D burn. Default from P7: in-house.
Cash discipline
No new plants in P5. HQ never below $100k floor. Clear Supplier Credit by end-P5. Hunt every quarterly subsidy. Minimize supplier credit reliance.
Preserve employment
All 5 inherited plants stay open through P5–P10 (SDG 8). 50% gender parity at C-level. Single-shift in P5 → kWh/unit reporting at P10 audit.
